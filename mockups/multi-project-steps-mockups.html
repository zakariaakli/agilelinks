A. Multi-Project Switcher

Three layout options for switching between active plans. All options show the most recently interacted plan by default, with quick access to other active plans.

Option A: Header Dropdown

Recommended

How it works

  • Compact switcher in the header, next to logo
  • Shows current plan name + milestone at a glance
  • Click to reveal dropdown with all active plans
  • Progress indicator on each plan

Trade-offs

Pros
  • + Minimal UI footprint
  • + Always accessible
  • + Works on mobile
  • + Familiar pattern (Linear, Slack)
Cons
  • - Extra click to see all plans
  • - Less visual at a glance

B. Steps Within Milestone

Two interaction patterns for managing steps. Steps are micro-commitments that live on the milestone level, with a maximum of 3 active at a time.

Option A: Inline Checklist

Recommended

How it works

  • Steps appear directly below milestone info
  • Simple checkbox interaction (click to complete)
  • Completed steps move to "Achievement Trail" below
  • AI suggestions appear in highlighted section
  • Max 3 active steps enforced visually

Trade-offs

Pros
  • + Everything visible at once
  • + Minimal clicks
  • + Clear progress feeling
  • + Mobile friendly
Cons
  • - Page can get long
  • - Achievement trail may be overlooked

Final Recommendation

Project Switcher

Header Dropdown (Option A)

Minimal footprint, works on mobile, familiar pattern from Linear/Slack.

Steps Display

Inline Checklist (Option A)

Everything visible, minimal clicks, clear progress feeling.

Step Limits

Max 3 active per milestone

Forces focus, prevents overwhelm, AI respects this limit.

Achievement Trail

Always visible below steps

Evidence of progress, motivation when feeling stuck.
